About Odderon Lab

Odderon Lab builds practical diagnostic tools for embedded engineers. We focus on subtle failures — power integrity issues, reversed polarity damage, unstable supply rails — the kinds of problems that quietly destroy projects.

Inspired by the rare physics phenomenon known as the odderon, our mission is to detect the subtle interactions others overlook.

Design Philosophy

  • No gimmicks
  • Clear technical documentation
  • Built for real-world prototyping
  • Engineer-first thinking
