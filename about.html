About Odderon Lab

Odderon Lab creates practical prototyping tools for embedded engineers. We build hardware that helps you validate, debug, and refine systems before they ship.

Inspired by the rare physics phenomenon known as the odderon, our mission is to detect the subtle interactions others overlook.

Design Philosophy

  • No gimmicks
  • Built for real-world prototyping
  • Engineer-first thinking
